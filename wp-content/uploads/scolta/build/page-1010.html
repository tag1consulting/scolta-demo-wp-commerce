About Terra Collecta

A Demonstration Site for Scolta Search Technology Terra Collecta is a premium e-commerce demonstration site showcasing Scolta, an intelligent search re-ranking technology for WooCommerce. The premise: 1,000 rare geological specimens — minerals, gemstones, fossils, and meteorites — browsable with natural language. Search for “something blue and sparkly for a gift” and find lapis lazuli, labradorite, blue apatite, and sapphire matrix. Search for “oldest thing you have” and find zircon crystals 4.4 billion years old. All products are fictional specimens existing only in our carefully curated imagination. No transactions are processed.
